Stoic Widgets

Privacy Policy

Effective May 21, 2026

Overview

Stoic Widgets is part of a family of related apps operated by the same developer, including Stoic Widgets, Dharma Widgets, and Buddhist Widgets. This policy explains what information the app handles, how that information is used, and what choices you have.

Stoic Widgets is designed to work offline first. Most app data is stored locally on your device unless you choose to sign in and use optional sync features.

Information We Handle

Depending on how you use the app, it may handle the following information:

  • display name
  • liked quotes
  • saved collections
  • followed topics
  • theme, wallpaper, and personalization preferences
  • reminder settings and streak data
  • widget configuration and widget preferences
  • subscription entitlement status needed to determine premium access

Sign In And Account Data

You can use the app without creating an account.

If you choose Sign in with Apple, Apple may provide your name and email address as part of that sign-in flow. The app uses Firebase Authentication to create or sign in to your account.

If you sign in, eligible app data may be stored in Firebase Firestore so you can restore it on another device.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions, free trials, renewals, cancellations, and purchase restoration are handled through Apple and RevenueCat.

We do not receive your full payment card information. We only handle the subscription and entitlement information needed to determine whether premium features should be available in the app and widget.

Permissions And Device Features

The app may request access to certain device features only when needed for the feature you choose to use.

  • Notifications, to send quote reminders and streak reminders if you enable them
  • Photos, to save exported quote wallpapers or quote images to your library
  • System share and clipboard features, when you explicitly choose to share or copy quote content

How Information Is Used

  • operate the app and its widgets
  • save your preferences and personalization choices
  • restore data across devices if you sign in
  • schedule and manage reminders you enable
  • determine premium access and subscription status
  • respond to support or privacy requests

Local Storage And Optional Sync

The app stores data locally on your device first. If you sign in, some app data may also sync to Firebase Firestore, including items such as your display name, liked quotes, collections, followed topics, theme selections, streak data, and reminder preferences.

Some data remains device-local and is not synced to Firestore.

  • widget configurations stored for the device widget extension
  • device-specific notification scheduling identifiers
  • other device-local operational state used only on that device

Sharing With Service Providers

We do not sell your personal data.

Information is shared only as needed with service providers that support the features you choose to use.

  • Apple, for Sign in with Apple, App Store billing, subscriptions, and platform services
  • RevenueCat, for subscription and entitlement management
  • Firebase, for authentication and optional cloud sync

Retention And Deletion

Locally stored app data remains on your device until you remove it, delete the app, or use the app's delete-data controls.

If you are signed in, synced account data may remain in Firebase until you delete your account through the app's account deletion flow, subject to platform reauthentication requirements.

Subscription and billing records are maintained by Apple and RevenueCat under their own policies and retention practices.

Your Choices

  • use the app without creating an account
  • choose whether to sign in
  • choose whether to enable reminders
  • choose whether to grant photo-library access
  • delete local app data from the device
  • delete synced account data through the app if signed in
  • disable notifications at any time in iOS settings

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the effective date above will be updated. Your continued use of the app after an updated policy becomes effective means the updated policy will apply going forward.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact stoicismwidget@yahoo.com.
